WHO WE ARE

We know that four years of college isn’t the right path for everyone. And we believe that no one should be excluded from the opportunity to experience a financially secure future — especially when a rewarding and stable career in healthcare could be just 12 to 24 weeks away.

Founded in 2010 in Austin, TX, Zollege Healthcare Career School offers hands-on, skills-based training programs designed to unlock a future in healthcare

Our accelerated programs in medical and dental assisting teach you everything you need to know to be career-ready sooner and are designed to fit into your life, not the other way around. When you enroll in one of our programs, you’ll experience hands-on, skills-based training and learn from working professionals in active medical and dental offices. Because our success is measured by yours, we offer personalized career guidance, externships and support as you seek to secure a position after completing the program.

At Zollege, we’re inspired to help build the next generation of healthcare workers and want to do everything we can to help you power your possible.

Who is Zollege?

Zollege is the parent company of a network of over 120 medical assistant and dental assistant school locations (and counting!) throughout the United States.

Our two programs are as follows:

Dental Assisting

Our 12-week dental assisting course provides comprehensive training via a combination of convenient, in-person weekend classes and flexible online lessons to prepare students for a successful career in the dental field.

Medical Assisting

Our 24-week medical assisting course offers an accelerated path to a career in healthcare via a combination of online learning and immersive, hands-on training to prepare students to become Certified Clinical Medical Assistants (CCMA).

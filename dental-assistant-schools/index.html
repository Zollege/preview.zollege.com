- Our Benefits -

Training the Next Generation of Skilled Dental Assistants

Hands-On & Online Learning

Our program features immersive, hands-on learning taught by experienced professionals. Students learn and practice in an actual dental office setting to acquire real-world experience.

Experienced Staff

We hire the most qualified and experienced instructors in town. All of our class instructors have several years of experience in the field and are passionate about sharing their skills and expertise with you.

Convenient Schedule

We offer a variety of convenient program locations and morning and evening class times, including Saturdays, to allow students to continue working while preparing for their new careers.

Scrubs & Supplies Included

Your tuition and fees include a set of scrubs and all supplies and materials needed for class. We also include your textbook at no extra charge, which will be provided to you digitally before class begins.

Flexible Payment Plans

Our learning model allows us to keep the cost of education lower than many traditional career schools, and we offer flexible payment plans to help you fit the cost of your training into your budget.

Career Services

We’re committed to helping our students throughout the program and after graduation. Your Student Advisor will be there to guide you during class, and to assist in your job search.

Become a Medical Assistant in 24 Weeks

Our 24-week medical assisting course offers an accelerated path to a career in healthcare via immersive, hands-on training to prepare students to become Certified Clinical Medical Assistants (CCMAs).

About Zollege

Zollege is the parent company of a network of over 120 medical assistant and dental assistant school locations (and counting!) throughout the United States. We founded Zollege in Austin, Texas, in 2010 to create affordable education, practical skills, and placement opportunities that allow students to pursue new careers while maintaining existing ones.

- Our Benefits -

Training the Next Generation of Skilled Medical Assistants

Hands-On Learning

Our program features immersive, hands-on learning taught by professional school instructors with years of experience in the field. Students learn and practice in an actual medical office setting.

Experienced Staff

We hire the most qualified and experienced instructors in town. All of our school instructors are either distinguished medical assistants or medical professionals.

Convenient Schedule

Many of our schools offer multiple class locations and times, including Saturdays. This allows flexibility for our students to continue working while being prepared for their next career.

Scrubs & Supplies Included

Your tuition includes a set of scrubs and all supplies and materials needed for class. The Certified Clinical Medical Assistants (CCMAs) guidebook is provided to each student at no additonal cost.

Affordable Tuition

Our learning model allows us to keep the cost of education extremely low. We offer standard and custom payment plans that do not require a credit check.

Career Services

We’re committed to helping our students during and after graduation. Once you graduate, our dedicated career services team will help you in your job search.

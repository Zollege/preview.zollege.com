OUR LOCATIONS

We strive to ensure each campus is rooted in a community that will provide job opportunities for these in-demand fields. We are proud to instruct students at over 100 campuses nationwide.

To apply, first find the right program at a location near you. Select the marker on the map to show the link to that program. Then submit your application to get started on your next career today!

Zollege Healthcare Career School offers hands-on, skills-based training programs that unlock accelerated pathways to a future in healthcare. Our 12-week dental and 24-week medical assistant programs combine interactive learning and real-world experience, delivering a comprehensive education that prepares students for the workplace.

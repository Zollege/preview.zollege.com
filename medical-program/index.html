Program Outcome: Prepare to Become a Certified Clinical Medical Assistant (CCMA)

Once all class requirements have been met, you’ll receive a Certificate of Completion from Zollege Healthcare Career School! You’ll enter the workforce with the knowledge, skills, and experience needed to begin a successful career as a medical assistant.

Additionally, upon passing the national exam through the National Healthcareer Association (NHA), you can become a Certified Clinical Medical Assistant (CCMA).*

*Laws vary by state. Upon completing the course, our team will work with you to ensure you get what you need to become a medical assistant. Zollege does not guarantee employment to students or graduates.

Medical Assistant Training Benefits

Many students go on to specialize in areas like optometry or podiatry and stack credentials by seeking certifications in phlebotomy and EKG administration. Professionals with credentials in multiple areas are in high demand among employers. The possibilities are endless once you’re fully trained as a medical assistant!

Financial Stability

Open the door to a life of financial freedom! Medical assistant employment is projected to grow 14% by 2032.

Job Stability & Security

Most medical assistants work full-time and enjoy predictable schedules, competitive pay, and benefits.

Valuable and Versatile

Kickstart your journey as a medical assistant. Becoming a CCMA can help you stand out in the field.

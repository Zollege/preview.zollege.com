What You'll Learn

Learning With a Purpose

The Zollege Dental Assistant Program prepares you with the skills, education, and confidence to launch your career in the dental field. As one of the leading schools for dental assistants, we connect learning and real-world experience through online, interactive lesson plans and labs taught in active dental facilities.

  • Chairside Duties
  • Temporary Crowns
  • Bleach Trays
  • Radiographs
  • Alginate Impressions
  • Diagnostic Models
  • Personal Protective Equipment
  • Operatory Setup/Breakdown
  • Sanitization & Sterilization Protocols
  • Dental Anatomy Components

Program Outcome: Start a Promising Career in the Dental Field

Once all class requirements have been met, you’ll receive a Certificate of Completion from Zollege Healthcare Career School. You’ll enter the workforce with the knowledge, skills, and experience needed to begin a successful career as a dental assistant.*

*Laws vary by state. Upon completing the course, our team will work with you to ensure you get what you need to become a dental assistant. Zollege does not guarantee employment to students or graduates.

Dental Assistant Training Benefits

Many students start as dental assistants and go on to specialize in areas such as orthodontics, oral surgery, or pediatrics or become hygienists and even dentists. The possibilities are endless once you’re fully trained as a dental assistant!

Financial Stability

Open the door to a life of financial freedom! Dental assistant employment is projected to grow 7% by 2032.

Job Stability & Security

Most dental assistants work full-time and enjoy predictable schedules, competitive pay, and benefits.

Valuable and Versatile

Kickstart your journey as a dental assistant. Gain valuable experience and support your education financially.
